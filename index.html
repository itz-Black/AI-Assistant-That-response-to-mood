About the Saarthi Project

The Problem: Isolation & Risk

Long-duration space missions subject astronauts to extreme psychological stressors: prolonged isolation, communication lag, and physical confinement. This environment elevates the risk of depression and anxiety, posing a threat to mission success.

Our Mission: A Digital Lifeline

Saarthi (meaning 'Charioteer') is designed as a constant, non-intrusive presence. Our mission is to provide immediate, personalized psychological support, acting as an early warning system and a tool for self-managed mental health.
